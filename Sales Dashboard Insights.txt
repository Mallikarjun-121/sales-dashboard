 Sales Dashboard Insights
 1. The West Region Leads in Sales
The West region consistently outperforms all others in total sales, indicating a strong customer base or higher demand in that geography.

 2. December Sees Peak Sales
There is a noticeable spike in sales every December, suggesting a seasonal trend — possibly due to holiday promotions or end-of-year purchases.

 3. Technology Category Dominates Revenue
Among the three main product categories, Technology generates the highest sales, contributing the largest share in the donut chart.

 4. Central Region Has High Sales but Lower Profit
Despite strong sales in the Central region, the profit margin is relatively lower, which may indicate high discounts or operational inefficiencies.

 5. Office Supplies Show Consistent Performance
While Office Supplies don’t lead in sales, they show steady and balanced performance across all regions, suggesting stable customer demand.
